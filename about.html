    About Vibe Check

    Vibe Check is a full-stack project made by Ben DeGraff, a CS Student at BYU. VibeCheck was created in a period of around 4 months and futhur development is TBD.

    Vibe Check's Goal

    Vibe Check aims to allow users to listen to music with their friends and family in a fair and shared way. Users can all suggests songs, chat with one another, and listen together. The experience is made to be shared, and enjoying music together is something that we beleive is very speical.
